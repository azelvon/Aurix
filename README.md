# Intermarket Market Analysis System

A market analysis dashboard focused on:
- Intermarket analysis
- USD strength monitoring
- Market synchronization
- Macro context
- Sentiment analysis
- Probabilistic scoring

The goal of this project is to simplify market complexity and help traders analyze market conditions more objectively.

---

# Features

## Intermarket Monitoring
Track relationships between:
- DXY
- XAUUSD
- Major forex pairs
- Bond yields

---

## USD Strength Analysis
Analyze overall USD strength using:
- major pair synchronization
- DXY momentum
- yield movement
- market context

---

## Market Synchronization Detection
Detect whether major pairs are moving in alignment with:
- bullish USD conditions
- bearish USD conditions
- neutral market conditions

---

## POI & Structure Analysis
Identify:
- support & resistance
- liquidity areas
- breakout zones
- market structure

---

## Market Scoring System
Simplify multiple market variables into a single contextual score.

Example:
- DXY breakout
- synchronized USD pairs
- rising yields
- gold rejection

→ stronger bullish USD score
